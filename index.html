Welcome to Exam Generator

Exam Generator is simple application for preparing exams with many amazing features.

!!! This is preview version. There are many key parts missing !!!

Our main idea:

Preparing exams may take a long time. When you want to prevent your students from copying, you can create two or three versions. But what if you could create a version for each of your students in a short period of time?

A few examples of things you can do with Exam Generator:

  • Create vocabulary tests
  • Pick a random question for a student
